## 10. Optimal Timing: Before vs After Earnings

### 10.1 Sell Premium BEFORE Earnings

**When it works best:**
- High IV Rank names where IV is already elevated (IV Rank > 50)
- Sectors with predictable IV spikes (Mega-Cap Tech, Industrials)
- Names where you can sell 5-10 days before and capture both IV expansion AND IV crush

**Best sectors for pre-earnings selling:**
1. **Fintech/Crypto** — Sell 7-10 days before. IV spikes from 70% to 100%+. Crush of 20-30 pts. Maximum profit potential.
2. **Growth/Tech (PLTR)** — Sell 7-10 days before. PLTR IV can spike from 55% to 80%. Crush of 12-18 pts.
3. **Semiconductors (NVDA)** — Sell 5-8 days before. NVDA IV spikes from 42% to 55%+. Crush of 10-15 pts.
4. **Mega-Cap Tech (META)** — Sell 5-7 days before. META IV spikes from 32% to 50%. Crush of 8-14 pts.

**Risk:** The stock can move MORE than the premium collected. This is the primary risk. Use defined-risk strategies (iron condors, credit spreads) to cap losses.

### 10.2 Sell Premium AFTER Earnings

**When it works best:**
- Names where the post-earnings move was smaller than expected (IV didn't crush enough)
- Sectors where IV remains elevated after earnings (Fintech/Crypto, Growth/Tech)
- When you want to avoid binary event risk entirely

**Best sectors for post-earnings selling:**
1. **Fintech/Crypto** — Even after the crush, IV remains at 50-90%. Still very expensive. Sell 1-3 days after.
2. **Semiconductors** — Post-crush IV of 35-55% is still elevated. Good for selling 2-5 days after.
3. **Growth/Tech** — PLTR post-crush IV of 50-65% is still rich. Sell 2-5 days after.
4. **Mega-Cap Tech** — Post-crush IV of 22-35% is moderate. Only sell if IV Rank > 45.

**Risk:** IV continues to compress after earnings. You miss the biggest part of the crush but also avoid the binary risk.

### 10.3 The Optimal Hybrid Strategy

**The "IV Expansion + Crush Capture" approach:**

| Timing | Action | Target IV | Strategy |
|--------|--------|-----------|----------|
| 10-14 days before | Enter position | IV starting to rise | Sell strangle or iron condor |
| 5-7 days before | Monitor IV | IV at peak | If IV > target, add to position |
| 1-2 days before | Decide | IV peaked | Hold through earnings OR close early |
| Day after earnings | Close | IV crushed | Close at 50% profit or let theta work |

**Decision rule:**
- If IV is already > 60th percentile when you enter (10-14 days out): HOLD THROUGH earnings to capture the crush
- If IV is < 40th percentile when you enter: CLOSE before earnings to avoid binary risk
- This is the "IV percentile filter" — only ride through earnings when premium is genuinely expensive
